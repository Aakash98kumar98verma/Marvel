Captain Amarica

Created in 1941 by Simon and Kirby. Transformed into a super soldier via serum in WWII. Iconic shield, indestructible vibranium. Patriotism symbol, red, white, blue costume. Leader of Avengers, strategic thinker. Thawed from ice in modern times. Moral, just, challenges authority. Chris Evans portrayed in MCU. Represents hope, resilience, justice. Legacy carried by others. Relationships with Iron Man, others. Comic legacy, pop culture icon.

Iron Man

Created by Stan Lee, first appeared in 1963. Genius inventor, built powered exosuit (Iron Man suit). Suit provides flight, super strength, weaponry. Philanthropist, runs Stark Industries. Struggles with heart injury, uses arc reactor. Played by Robert Downey Jr. in MCU. Founding member of the Avengers. Deals with personal demons, growth over time. Sacrifices himself to save the universe (MCU).

Thor

Thor is aprominent Marvel character based on Norse mythology. He's Asgardian God of Thunder, wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir ,which grants him power and control over lightning. Thor, often portrayed as brave and noble,has appeared in comics, movies, and TV shows. The character is known for his strength, resilience, and iconic red cape.

Hulk

The Hulk, created by Marvel, is the alter ego of Dr.Bruce Banner. When Banner experiences extreme stress or anger, he transforms into the Hulk, a massive, green-skinned creature with incredible strength and durability. The Hulk's physical power is nearly limitless, and driven nature often makes controlling him a challenge. Despite his destructive tendencies, Hulk occasionally allies with other Marvel heroes, including the Avengers. Hulk is a more intelligent and emotionally complex figure known as "Professor Hulk.

Thanos

Thanos, a Marvel supervillain, is one of the most formidable adversaries in the Marvel Universe. Created by Jim Starlin. Thanos is obsessed with the concept of death and seeks to impress Mistress Death, the personification of death itself. He is known for his immense strength, durability, and genius-level intellect. Thanos wields the Infinity Gauntlet, a powerful artifact that grants him control overall six Infinity Stones. He's a major antagonist in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, driving the events of "Avengers: Infinity War" and "Avengers :Endgame".

Nick Fury

Nick Fury is prominent character in the Marvel Universe,known for his leadership and involvement in intelligence operations. He's often depicted as the head of S.H.I.E.L.D. a covert organization that deals with superhuman threats and global security issues. Fury is recognized for his distinctive eyepatch, grizzled demeanor, and no-nonsense attitude. He's a skilled strategist, a master of espionage, and has connections throughout the superhero community.

SpiderMan

Spider-Man also known as Peter Parker, he's a young, agile and quick written character who gains extraordinary powers of radioactive spider. With his iconic red and blue suit, Spiderman swing through the streets of New york, using his suberhuman strength, agility and web shooting abilities to fight crime and protact the innocent. He's known for his 6th sences. He is marvel most enduring and relatable character.
